FAQ

Common questions

Do mini splits require ductwork?
No. That's one of the main advantages — mini splits are ductless, making them ideal for homes without existing duct systems or for additions and retrofits.
Can a mini split handle both heating and cooling?
Yes. Most modern mini splits are heat pumps, meaning they provide both heating and cooling in one system. This is a major advantage in the Pacific Northwest.
Are there rebates available for mini splits?
Often yes. Puget Sound Energy and Snohomish PUD offer rebates on qualifying heat pump mini splits. Federal tax credits may also apply. Ask your installer for current offers.
Is this an exact quote?
No. This gives you a realistic local range based on homes and installs similar to yours. A specialist can confirm the exact number.
Will I need to talk to someone right away?
No. You get your estimate first. Speaking with a specialist is always optional.
